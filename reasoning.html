Would this really work?

Obviously, with an idea this brilliant, The question that arises is whether this would work or not. The short answer to this question is yes, and the long answer is of course. The absence of humans and their technology would reduce the methane (which is one of the most abundant greenhouse gas) being released annually in the world by approximately 285-370 million metric tons and Carbon Dioxide by 40 Billion metric tons, which means easy net zero emissions globally and the average temperature of the earth would drop back to normal, essentially saving the planet from destruction.
